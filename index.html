WEB-BASED AR FEATURE TRACKER

Instructions
1. Upload an RGB image.
2. Generate grayscale image and detect features.
3. Save and export the processed image as your marker.
4. Print the marker image on A4 paper.
5. Launch AR and point your camera at the marker to see the 3D object.
Note: The AR scene uses the marker image you saved - point your camera at the printed marker!
